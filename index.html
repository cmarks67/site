How it fits into your operation

No major systems project. You use exports and data you already have. Tools are designed to sit alongside your WMS, not replace it.

1

Export the data you already trust

Pull simple CSV / Excel exports from WMS, time & attendance, and finance. No direct system access or API work required.

2

Drop into a ready-built tool

Paste your data into a structured template or connect to a lightweight database. The logic, calculations, and views are already built.

3

Use outputs in daily decisions

Use outputs in stand-ups, client reviews, and budget discussions. When the operation changes, adjust the assumptions – not the whole model.
